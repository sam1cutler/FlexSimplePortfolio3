ABOUT

I'm a Seattle-based Javascript developer with experience building beautiful, intuitive apps using React, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL. I've also spent time as a biology researcher and teacher, primarily at the college level. While conducting my PhD research, I used a lot of Python, Bash, and R, and found that I loved programming, creating highly functional digital tools to improve life for myself and my colleagues in lab.

With my background, I've developed great skills in problem-solving, troubleshooting, team collaboration, and communicating technical material to any audience. I am quick to learn and have great attention to detail, and I love to take on complex challenges.

Outside of coding, my main passions all take advantage of the Pacific Northwest outdoors, especially rock climbing, backpacking, and skiing.
